"Happiness"

Through your cheerful memories, you entered a warmth of shining lights. Euphrosyne, the goddess of joy and merriment welcome you with open arms.

Euphrosyne is a goddess of good cheer, joy and mirth. Her name is the female version of the word euphrosynos, "merriment". Pindar wrote that these goddesses were created to fill the world with pleasant moments and good will.

She offer you a gift.

Open your gift.

You were gifted a puppy, You are more joyful then ever. But you feel sadness when looking at it, maybe your own pet has passed away in the past. You also feel a sense of anger, perhaps this dog troubled you in someway.
