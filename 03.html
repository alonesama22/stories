"Fear"

Through this feaful memory, you found yourself slowly climbing up a red mountain that is dripping down blood. At the peak, you see an amphora with engraving of warriors in a chariot. You take a closer look at the symbols.

This is Phobos, the god of fear and panic.

Phobos is the god and personification of fear and panic in Greek mythology. Phobos was the son of Ares and Aphrodite, and the brother of Deimos. In Classical Greek mythology, Phobos exists as both the god of and personification of the fear brought by war.
