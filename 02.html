"Sadness"

Entering your unhappy memories, ahead of you is a big statue in a dark room. You recognized this statue.

This is Achlys, the goddess of misery and saddness.

Achlys is one of the figures depicted on Heracles' shield, representing the personification of sorrow. In Homer, Achlys is the mist which fogs or blinds mortal eyes. Her Roman counterpart Caligo was said to have been the mother of Chaos.

There is an item on her hand.

Grab the item.

The item disappear as you touch it and it turned into nothing. You feel depressed and empty inside. You are surprise by this, and now sense fear because the goddess' statue has vanish, leaving you alone in the dark. Because of this, you have triggered another memory.
